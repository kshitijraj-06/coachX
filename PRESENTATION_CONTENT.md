## Presentation Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme
- **Primary:** #6C63FF (Purple)
- **Secondary:** #9C88FF (Light Purple)
- **Background:** Dark gradient (#0F0F23 to #1A1A2E)
- **Text:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent:** #4ECDC4 (Teal)

### Typography
- **Headers:** Poppins Bold (24-32px)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (16-18px)
- **Captions:** Inter Light (12-14px)

### Visual Elements
- **Icons:** Material Design icons
- **Images:** App screenshots, mockups
- **Charts:** Clean, minimal design
- **Animations:** Subtle transitions

### Layout Principles
- **Consistency:** Uniform spacing and alignment
- **Hierarchy:** Clear information structure
- **Whitespace:** Adequate breathing room
- **Focus:** One main point per slide
